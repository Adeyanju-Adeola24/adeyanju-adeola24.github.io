Problem

The Problem

Campus commerce is broken. Students buy and sell across scattered WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories, and Telegram channels. There's no trust, no structure, no accountability. Scams are common. Good vendors can't build reputations. Buyers have no protection.

Fragmented

WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram — students juggle 3-5 platforms just to buy and sell on campus.

No Trust

No verified vendors, no reviews, no escrow. Scams are the #1 reason students avoid online campus commerce.

No Structure

No storefronts, no inventory management, no analytics. Vendors operate blindly across chaotic chat threads.

Wasted Time

Hours spent answering the same questions, negotiating across platforms, and chasing payments.

Solution

The Solution

Hollap brings it all together — verified vendors, reviews, real-time chat, escrow protection, and your daily command center: the Board. One platform for campus commerce, communication, and productivity.

Markets

Discover products, chat with vendors, read reviews, and buy with confidence.

Board

Your daily command center. Orders, messages, updates, trending products — all in one place.

MI Assistant

AI-powered smart chat summaries, auto-responses, and purchase recommendations.

Escrow

Payment held securely until delivery confirmation. Trust built in, from day one.
